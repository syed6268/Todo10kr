# Goal: Cold reach out to 10 seed-stage investors

- **Horizon:** 1 week
- **Priority:** 2
- **Category:** career / fundraising

## Why this goal matters
Direct supplement to the YC application goal: warm investor signal in the
deck materially improves application strength.

## Definition of done
- 10 personalized cold emails sent to seed-stage AI-focused investors
- Each email references something specific about the recipient's recent
  writing, portfolio, or talks (no copy-paste blasts)
- 2+ replies → meetings booked

## Constraints
- Maximum 2 sends per day to avoid burn-out and to allow personalization
- No paid email tools — manual sends from primary inbox
- Use the in-app cold-email playbook (`cold_email_guide`) for templates
